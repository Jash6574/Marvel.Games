About Marvel Games

P P Savani University's Marvel Games is where schools showcase their sports talent and kindle a fiery passion for the game. With diverse indoor and outdoor sports, it's about skills, teamwork and the true essence of sportsmanship. Beyond medals, we aim to fuel a lasting love for sports, molding passionate individuals who shine on the field. Join us at Marvel Games, where every athlete's fervor lights up the game. Marvel Games aims to instill a lasting passion for sports in every participant, shaping them into well-rounded individuals who appreciate the value of discipline, resilience, and the thrill of the game. Join us at PPSU Marvel Games, where we believe that every school has a champion waiting to emerge.

General Guidelines

  • The PPSU Marvel Games is an inter-school multi-sport event designed to offer young talents an opportunity to demonstrate their sporting prowess. To participate in this exciting competition, schools are required to complete the registration process using the provided QR code. Please note that only teams with valid registrations through their respective school authorities will be considered for participation.
  • Each player is allowed to participate in a maximum of two events. However, if a player fails to arrive at the venue for their scheduled match on time, their opponent will receive a walkover victory.
  • The event or sports competition will only proceed if there are a minimum of 15 registrations.
  • The teams with maximum overall points at the completion of all events of PPSU Marvel Games will be declared as “Champion of PPSU Marvel Games”.
    Point table: -
    Event Winner Runner-up Second runner-up
    Team event 2000 1000 500
    Individual event 1000 500 250
  • Each participant is required to bring their School I-card issued by the school along with a I- card issued by government. In case of unavailability of the school I-card, they are required to bring a Bonafide certificate, with their photograph and School seal (half on photograph and half on certificate).
  • Team should report 30 minutes before start of match. Any team late than 10 minutes of the scheduled time will have to forfeit the match.
  • The referee decision will be the final decision during the game. In case of a conflict, decisions of Sports Committee will be considered as final decision.
  • Protests, if any, against the decision of the technical officials shall be made in writing to the organizer at least within 60 minutes of the incident with a protest fee of Rs.5,000/- which shall be refunded if the appeal is upheld. The decision of the jury committee shall be FINAL and BINDING on all the participants.
  • The entire tournament will be held as per the schedule and time decided by The PPSU Sports Committee well in advance. There will be no re-scheduling of any event; in case of withdrawal, the opposing team will receive a walkover.
  • The March Past of participants in proper sports attire will be held during the Opening Ceremony at 9:30 am on 26/10/2023. If any team not participating in the march Past, will be disqualified.
  • Parents, Coaches, Managers and unauthorized persons are strictly prohibited in the playing area during the matches.
  • Schedule of the tournament will be displayed on the notice board placed at the venue and the Tournament. website,
  • Fixtures will be drawn by tournament committee on October23, 2023 at 12 noon and will be uploaded on the website on October 24 2023 at 12 noon.
  • Maintaining discipline at the venues will be the sole responsibility of Team Captains, Managers and Coaches/ PETs.
  • Proper sports attire according to respective sports will only be allowed for participating in any sports event.
  • The incidents of the indiscipline will be reported to the Principal of the Concerned School For necessary action.
  • All participants are expected to behave in a civil and decent fashion while on the PPSU Campus, both on and off the field, and abide by the rules of the campus.
  • PPSU Sports Committee will not bear any expenses of food and accommodation of visiting teams. Participating schools will have to bear all the other expenses.
  • By participating in the event, you agree that Sports Committee and PPSU is not responsible for any loss, damage, injury, or death during, before or after the event.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to modify, reschedule, or cancel the event under unforeseen circumstances, without the obligation to provide explanations to the participants.
  • The modified rules will be informed by Referee before start of games.
  • For any queries you can mail or contact us at: sports.officer@ppsu.ac.in, +91 9512 035 689
