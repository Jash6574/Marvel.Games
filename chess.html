CHESS



General Instructions

  • ID cards issued by school of every team member are compulsory in order to play/participate. If any player does not have an id card, then they need to carry his/her school bonafide certificate.
  • Team should report 30 minutes before the start of the match.
  • Any team later than 10 minutes of the scheduled time will have to forfeit the match.
  • Any team found misbehaving with the referee or the member of the organizing committee will be disqualified.
  • Any team player prior/later found having a fake ID committee’s decision will be final.
  • The referee's decision will be the final decision during the game.
  • In case of a conflict, decisions of the Sports Committee will be considered as final decision.
  • Participants must be under 18 years old, with a birthdate on or after October 29, 2005.
  • Participants must be currently enrolled as students in either a school or a diploma institute.
  • Teams have to play in sports wear as per their respective games.
  • Every Team have to wear same colored jersey with School name on it.

General Instructions

  • Composition of the Team:
      Mixed Event (no separate Girls Event) –2 students are required which can comprise of both males & females in the team.
      Players will face each other in a one-on-one matchup, the winner advances to the next round, and the loser exits the tournament, Send entries, the names of all the players with their respective FIDE ratings, if any, should be given to enable the committee to arrive at the teams’ average rating for ranking purposes.
      These names and ratings should be reconfirmed in person at the Technical Meeting.
      The Board Order could be decided by the respective team Captain/Manager, before the commencement of the championship.
  • System of Play:
      The system of play will be Knockout system.
  • Time Control:
      The match time will be of 30 minutes, Chess Clocks will be used in last 10 minutes if required. Time control will be 3 Minutes + 02 second time increment.
      Players need to press the clock with the same hand with which they moved the piece. The opposing team/player may ask the opponent to wait 10 secs if this rule is violated.
  • Standings & Tie Break:
      For win of the player will get advances to the next round.
      If the game’s result will be draw, new game will be played by the players. The time control will 3 minutes with no increment.
  • In case of tiebreak,
      • Rankings will be decided from the last remain players.
      Arbiter decision will be the final decision.
      Clock order and board order will be decided by arbiter only.
      Illegal 2 will be the loss of the player.
